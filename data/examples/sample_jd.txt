Data Scientist - Financial Analytics
XYZ Capital Partners | New York, NY

About the Role:
We are seeking a Data Scientist to join our Financial Analytics team. You will
build and maintain predictive models, data pipelines, and analytical tools that
drive investment decisions. The ideal candidate has strong Python skills,
experience with machine learning, and familiarity with financial data.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and deploy machine learning models for financial prediction and risk analysis
- Build and optimize data pipelines (ETL) for processing large-scale financial datasets
- Collaborate with portfolio managers and research analysts to translate business
  questions into analytical solutions
- Implement NLP-based analysis of earnings calls, SEC filings, and financial news
- Design and maintain dashboards and automated reporting systems
- Conduct statistical analysis and hypothesis testing to validate model performance

Requirements:
- Master's degree in Statistics, Computer Science, Data Science, or related quantitative field
- 0-3 years of experience in data science, machine learning, or quantitative research
- Strong proficiency in Python (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, PyTorch or TensorFlow)
- Experience with SQL and large-scale data processing (Spark, PySpark preferred)
- Familiarity with NLP techniques and LLM-based applications
- Experience with cloud computing or HPC environments
- Strong communication skills and ability to present findings to non-technical stakeholders

Nice to have:
- Experience with financial data (10-K filings, earnings calls, market data)
- Familiarity with RAG pipelines or vector databases
- Experience with Docker/Kubernetes for model deployment
- Knowledge of time-series analysis and forecasting
